About

Altair Technology was founded in 1993 with the goal of empowering the small to mid-size enterprise with technologies and tools that have enabled large organizations to achieve impressive productivity gains with these e-tools.

These technologies and electronic tools (e-Tools) have historically only been available to organizations large enough to have Information Technology staffs with the expertise and resources to research, choose, deploy and support new technologies. Altair's mission is to make these new tools available to all businesses at affordable costs.

Altair Consultants work in partnership with client management to select the e-Tools that make sense for your business. Once a technology solution is deployed Altair is ready to play the support role that makes the most sense for your company.

Experienced staff can manage your applications and networks through remote management consoles to ensure that your information systems are running properly all the time.

Altair is ready to host your data and applications on one of our Azure Servers. Altair communication and application experts make sure that your information systems are running and error-free. Daily (or hourly) backups, Uninterruptible Power Supplies, Broad-band secure communications, wireless access, high-performance computers at a cost significantly below operating in-house make Altair hosting an increasingly popular option.

If you are contemplating a website or development project, Altair has the demonstrable expertise to get you there. Contact us for your consultation.
